// the platform

Four AI systems, one recruiting pipeline built for trucking.

Generic hiring tools don't understand a driver. Franklins AI is built around what actually matters in trucking — endorsements, equipment, routes, compliance, and whether a driver will still be there in six months.

01 — Screening

NLP resume screening

Reads each application the way a veteran recruiter would — CDL class, ELD experience, endorsements, route and equipment history — and filters for real fit, not keywords.

02 — Interviews

24/7 AI interviews & qualification

An AI recruiter engages every applicant instantly, at any hour, asks the right questions, confirms availability and requirements, and passes only genuinely ready candidates to a human.

03 — Retention

Predictive retention analytics

Scores each candidate on likelihood to stay, so carriers hire for retention — attacking the turnover that quietly drains fleet profitability.

04 — One report

Unified vetting report + ATS

CDL, MVR, carrier history and FMCSA compliance in one clear report, delivered straight into the carrier's existing ATS — no rip-and-replace.

// the methodology

A five-step rollout, not a plug-in you hope works.

AI recruiting only pays off when it's built into how a carrier actually operates. Franklins AI follows a structured method — measured against KPIs at every step.

1

Audit

Map the current recruiting workflow and find where drivers and hours leak out.

2

Set KPIs

Define measurable targets — time-to-hire, cost-per-hire, retention.

3

Integrate

Deploy the AI stack into the existing ATS and carrier tools — no rip-and-replace.

4

Pilot

Run a controlled 3–6 month pilot on live driver hiring.

5

Measure & train

Prove the numbers, retrain the model, and upskill the HR team.
